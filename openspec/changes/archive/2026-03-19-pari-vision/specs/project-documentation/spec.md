## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Project README
The project SHALL have a README at the root that gives any reader an immediate sense of what Pari is, why it exists, and how to get started.

#### Scenario: New visitor lands on the repo
- **WHEN** anyone opens the repository for the first time
- **THEN** the README SHALL communicate what Pari is, who it is for, and what problem it solves within the first screen

### Requirement: Vision document
The project SHALL have a vision document that articulates the long-term direction, core beliefs, design philosophy, and non-negotiables of Pari — all in one place.

#### Scenario: New contributor wants to understand intent
- **WHEN** a contributor reads the vision document
- **THEN** they SHALL understand the "why" behind Pari's existence, its core beliefs, design principles, and what it explicitly chooses not to be

#### Scenario: Design decision alignment
- **WHEN** a contributor faces a design decision
- **THEN** the vision document SHALL serve as a reference to evaluate options against Pari's foundational values

### Requirement: Target audience document
The project SHALL document who Pari is for — describing the teams, roles, and contexts where Pari is the right fit, and where it is not.

#### Scenario: Evaluating fit
- **WHEN** a team lead evaluates whether Pari is right for their team
- **THEN** the target audience document SHALL give them enough context to make that decision without needing to dig into the implementation
